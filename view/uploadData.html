Welcome to the first step of focus2hit!


Before anything else, we need you to upload here your historical data. With this data, we will build for you a predictor model to learn how your employees have behaved in the past. Make sure this data belongs to you and you can use it accordingly to LOPD.
To make sure your predictor is the best we can build for you, try to add all attributes you can to complete your own data.
Don't worry if you don't have all.

The optimal attributes needed were:

Name Attribute Description Type of attribute Mandatory?
EmployeeID Integer identifying an employee Integer No
Record id Integer identifying a unique row of our final dataset Integer No
Hire date Date of hire of the employee Date No
Record date Date of the registration of the employee in the dataset Date No
Termination date Date of termination of the employee (it can be a default date if he's still working Date No
Length of service Integer expressing the years from hiring to record date Integer No
Age Integer referring to the age of the employee Integer No
Target Boolean expressing our class value. It can assume value 1 if an employee is likely to leave, 0 otherwise Boolean Yes
Other target fields List of other fields related to our target value List No
Job title Role of the employee in the organization String No
Salary Monthly income of an employee Integer No
Special field types Contact us to add another fields List No
